Frequently asked questions

Loop is a weekly collaboration note. No feeds. No swiping. Just one introduction at a time.

What is Loop?

Loop is an email-first service that introduces you to one local musician each week based on your goals, style, and availability.

Is there an app?

No — on purpose. Loop lives in your inbox. Fewer introductions. More room to actually connect.

How does matching work?

We use your survey answers (location, goals, collaboration style, commitment level, genres/influences) to find good-fit pairings. You’ll typically get one introduction per week.

Do you share photos?

By default, no. Profiles are text-first so collaboration stays about process and intention, not comparison.

Can I pause or unsubscribe?

Yes. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. Later we’ll add a “pause” option too.

How do I contact you?

Email hello@loop.fm.
