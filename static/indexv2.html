What is Loop?

Loop is a slow alternative to social apps.

Instead of browsing profiles, you receive one short introduction each week to another local musician.

No photos. No feeds. You decide what happens next.

How it works

  1. 1. Sign up and answer a short survey.
  2. 2. Receive one intro per week.
  3. 3. Reach out if it feels right.
